Metre (m)

In Length conversion Metre unit is m.The SI unit of length conversion is metre. Metre is thousand times of millimetre. Thus the formula of metre to millimetre conversion is

$$m ={mm \text{ } * \text{ } 1000}$$

For example: If we have to convert 15.25 metre to millimetre, then

$$=15.25m$$

$$={15.25 \text{ } * \text{ } 1000}$$

$$=1525$$

Millimetre (mm)

In length conversion Millimetre unit is mm. The SI unit of length conversion is metre. Millimetre is one thousandth times of metre. Thus the formula of millimetre to metre conversion is

$$mm ={m \over 1000}$$

For example: If we have to convert 1525 millimetre to metre, then

$$=1525mm$$

$$={1525 \over 1000}$$

$$=15.25$$
